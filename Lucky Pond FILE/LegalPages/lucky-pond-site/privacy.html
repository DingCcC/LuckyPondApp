Lucky Pond Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Lucky Pond handles information for the iOS game experience.

01

Information stored by the game

Lucky Pond stores gameplay progress, settings, resources, fish records, gear, pond decor, event progress, and achievements on your device.

The app does not require an account and does not collect personal information directly through gameplay.

02

Support email

If you contact support, we receive the email address and information you choose to include in your message. This information is used only to respond to your request.

03

Permissions and platform services

Device permission choices are managed by iOS, including the App Tracking Transparency system prompt when requested. Lucky Pond does not specify advertising, analytics, account, purchase, or location service providers in this policy.

  • Apple platform services may be used for app distribution and permission management.
  • You can manage app permissions in device settings.
04

Retention and choices

Support messages are kept only as long as reasonably necessary to respond, resolve issues, and meet applicable obligations.

You may request access, correction, or deletion of information associated with your support message by emailing us.

05

Children and updates

Lucky Pond is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
